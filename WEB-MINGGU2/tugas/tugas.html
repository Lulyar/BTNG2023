IT'S NOT JUST CHICKEN

Sweet Madame is a food item that the player can cook. The recipe for Sweet Madame is available from the start of the game. Depending on the quality, Sweet Madame restores 20/22/24% of Max HP and an additional 900/1,200/1,500 HP to the target character. The ingredients to make it are also easy, you only need fowl (fresh poultry meat. When processed well, it can be made into a delicious dish) and sweet flower (a very fragrant flower. It can be found even in dark places because of its distinctive aroma.)
